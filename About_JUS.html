Warbixin ku saabsan Jaamacadda Ummadda Soomaaliyeed

Jaamacadda Ummadda Soomaaliyeed waxaa la aas-aasay, sanad-ka marka uu ahaa bishii sebtembar 1954-ki mac-had ahaan.

socdo...

1-12-1969-kii machadkii loo bedelo Jaamacadda ummadda soomaaliyeed,Burburkii soomaliya soo wajahay Dhowr iyo labaatan sano ka dib waxaa lagu guuleystay in dib loo furo JUS oo ahayd hooyadii waxbarashada dalka. Sanadku markuu ahaa 16-8-2014-kii ayaa guddigii sare ee JUS oo MD xasan sheekh madax u ahaa waxay shaaciyeen in JUS dib loo furayo arday cusubna ay qaadan doonto. 17-9-2014-kii ayeyna toos ubilaabatay duruusta jaamacadda.

Faracyada JUS

Gaheyr

Waa Faraca ugu wayn faracyada JUS ku dhawaad 6 kulliyad ayay leedahay, kulliyad walbana waxa ay leedahay departments ayada u gaar ah.

KM4

KM4 waa qayb kale oo ay jaamacaddda leedahay waxa ay ku taal L4 waa meel aad u qurux badan. kulliyado kala duwanna lagu qaato.

Shabeelle

Shabeelle waa xarun ay leedahay jaammacadda, kulliyadaha kuma badno, laakiin xafiisyada JUS ayaa ku yaal waana xarunta 3aad.

Vision

SNU aspires to be a world class public university that excels in education, innovation, research, community outreach and technology transfer and developing the world’s most promising students and outstanding staff to benefit society as whole.

Mission

SNU strives to provide relevant education and research programs to produce the required professionals to human resource necessary for addressing the socioeconomic, political, cultural, science and technology needs of the country.
